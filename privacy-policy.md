# Privacy Policy for CallChooser

CallChooser does not collect, store, or share personal data with third parties.

## Permissions
The app may request access to:
- Contacts (to display contact names)
- Call logs (to show recent calls)
- Microphone (only for voice search functionality)

All data is processed locally on the device and is never transmitted to external servers.

## Data Sharing
No personal data is shared with third parties.

## Ads
This app does not contain advertisements.

## Children
This app is not designed for children under 13.
